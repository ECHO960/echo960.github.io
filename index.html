Fragments of My Mind

  • Deep learning is cool! But if we define more and more difficult tasks and apply deep network to solve it, can we get to the point of strong AI? By now, it seems that logical-based AI is beat by data-based AI by now. Well, if we stick to define-problem -> making data -> choosing algorithm procedure, data-driven methods are good. But what if we want more? What if we want a algorithm can learn beyond well-defined questions?
  • I am more interested in vision because interpreting visual information is more difficult and crucial. The thing on my mind is still how we store our visual image. When you open your eyes, are you calculating 1024*768 items? Segmentation may be a naive solution. But is there a high-level 3D coding method that behave like human, and possible used by future Android for information input?
